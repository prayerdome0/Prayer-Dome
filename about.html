Who We Are

Prayer Dome is a Christ-centered ministry committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, transforming lives through prayer, and raising believers who walk in faith, holiness, and the power of the Holy Spirit. Founded by Pastor Seedwell Masuku, the ministry was established with a vision to reach nations, plant churches, and bring people closer to God through sincere worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

At Prayer Dome, we believe that prayer is the foundation of spiritual growth and breakthrough. We are passionate about creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God, grow spiritually, and discover their purpose in Christ. Through regular prayer gatherings, worship services, Bible studies, evangelism, and discipleship programs, we aim to strengthen individuals, families, and communities.

Our ministry is built on the teachings of the Holy Bible and guided by Christian values of love, unity, integrity, humility, and service. We believe in salvation through Jesus Christ, water baptism, the work of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and holy living according to the Word of God.

Prayer Dome is also dedicated to community impact through compassionate outreach. We believe the Church should not only preach the Gospel but also demonstrate God's love by supporting widows, orphans, youth, and vulnerable people in society.
